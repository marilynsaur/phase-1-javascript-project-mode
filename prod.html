SantaFe Train

This train represents the spirit of the Wild West and includes the cowcatcher, the Diamond smokestack, large headlight and interesting color scheme.

  • Track length:13,115 miles (21,107 km)
  • Freight carried:59,565,100 tons
  • Passengers carried:11,264,000

Despite the name, its main railroad never served Santa Fe, New Mexico. This was because the terrain was too rough. A branch line from Lamy reached Santa Fe instead.
